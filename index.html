Comprehensive Machinery Installation Solutions

AL WEJHA GROUP provides end-to-end machinery installation services for industrial plants, construction sites, and manufacturing facilities throughout Oman. Our certified technicians ensure precise installation according to manufacturer specifications and international safety standards.

1

Site Assessment & Preparation

Detailed evaluation of installation site including foundation inspection, power requirements, and safety considerations. We handle all preparatory work including concrete bases and anchor bolt installation.

2

Equipment Receiving & Inspection

Careful unloading and verification of all machinery components against shipping manifests. Damage assessment and documentation before installation begins.

3

Precision Assembly

Methodical assembly of machine components using specialized tools and lifting equipment. Includes alignment checks and pre-operation testing.

4

Power & Utility Connections

Professional connection to electrical systems, hydraulics, pneumatics, and other utilities according to Omani regulations and manufacturer guidelines.

5

Testing & Commissioning

Comprehensive operational testing under load conditions. Performance verification and final adjustments before handover to client.
